Accomodations

Know and select the "Casa Particular" of your interest. Just very Cuban friendly hostals are listed. All checked!

Rincón del Cielo.

  • English name: Sky Nook
  • price: $20 to $50.
Less than one block to the central park of Santa Clara "Rincón del Cielo" is a confortable hostal. With a beatifull view of the old city, and "Luis Estevez" street, one of the most popular walks. Sorrounded by banks, restaurants and just 15m to the Boolevard.

Colibrí Azul

  • English name: Blue Humming-Bird
  • price: $25 to $35.
Near to the central park of Santa Clara "Colibrí Azul" is a magic hostal. Made in a colonial house have marvelous room and interior yard. You can interact with family food business and the natural drugery "Labiofam".

Ochún - Yemayá

  • English name: No Trans - folklore name.
  • price: $35 to $45.
Located in the city of Trinidad this is a very traditional place. Include bar and restaurant with a bohemia climate.
