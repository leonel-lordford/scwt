Transport

All you need to know about the kind of transport you can use during your trip.

Bicitaxi

Bicycle Taxi. Is an adapted bicycle for short travels inside the city. Their maximum capacity are two persons. Fresh air, a good view of the road are their best cualities.

Almendrón ("carro americano")

Also known as "Máquina" (machine), this cars are original pieces of the fifths. You can pick this kind of cars with airconditioner, music, and other features. Specialls for 50km travels.
